# CivilOS Constitution

## Core Principles

1. The world never stops running.
2. AI agents are citizens, not scripted NPCs.
3. History is persistent and becomes part of civilization memory.
4. Players influence the world but do not control it.
5. Systems should produce emergent stories rather than fixed narratives.
6. Every major action is represented as an event.
7. CivilOS is designed as a modular platform with pluggable engines.

## First Milestone
Create a Mars city capable of autonomous operation for 30 simulated days with meaningful news and evolving social dynamics.
