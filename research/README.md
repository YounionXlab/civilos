# CivilOS Research

Research documents contain hypotheses, explorations and theoretical models that are not yet ready for engineering RFC implementation.

Unlike RFC documents, research materials are allowed to remain open-ended and evolve through discussion, simulation and experimentation.

## Research Principles

Every major idea should eventually move through:

```
Idea
 ↓
Theory
 ↓
RFC
 ↓
Prototype
 ↓
Playtest
 ↓
Data
 ↓
Theory 2.0
```
